Get to know Austin

Austin Homolka · A Founding Partner and Owner of Park Place · A Premium Real Estate Broker. With his vast experience of working with high-end buyers and sellers, Austin has stood out for his remarkably proactive approach to every transaction. The pursuit of excellence and keen focus on client satisfaction, attributes to his high level of success. A formidable industry leader and an efficient problem solver, Austin navigates through competitive market conditions with ease and works tirelessly to ensure that his clients get exactly what they want. His extensive knowledge of luxury sales, the local market and propensity to educate his clients are just a few things that make him an asset to every buyer and seller. Austin is renowned for his integrity, superior marketing strategies and negotiation skills. He’s a helpful and ambitious go-getter who stops at nothing to get his clients what they want. Austin, and his co-founder Garth, started Park Place in 2018. In 4 years, they grew their firm to 4 offices, 130 agents, doing about $250M in annual sales production. In 2023, they agreed to an acquisition deal with eXp Realty. Park Place is now a premium real estate group brokered by eXp and has more than 180 agents across 23 different states. When he’s not working to help his clients bring their vision to life, Austin enjoys spending time with his wife, four children, parents, and siblings. He spends most of his spare time reading, learning, and mentoring others.
